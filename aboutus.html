The Swift Keys Story

Established in 2000 in North London, Swift Keys emerged from a simple idea: to revolutionize the luxury car rental experience in the UK. Our journey began when our founder, after experiencing numerous frustrations with traditional car hire services, decided to create something truly different

2000

Launched with 3 premium vehicles in London

2003

Introduced our signature concierge service and expanded to 15 vehicles

2005

Pioneered contactless delivery and digital rental agreements

2010

Expanded to Birmingham and Manchester with 40+ vehicles

2024

Growing fleet of 75+ vehicles and launching eco-friendly options
